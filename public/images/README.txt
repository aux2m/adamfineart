ADAM FINE ART - IMAGE FOLDER GUIDE
===================================

DROP YOUR IMAGES IN THESE FOLDERS:

/logo/
  - logo.png (or .jpg, .svg) - Your main logo for the header
  - favicon.ico - Browser tab icon (optional)

/hero/
  - hero-bg.jpg - Main hero background image (recommended: 1920x1080 or larger)

/portfolio/murals/
  - Add your mural photos here
  - Name them descriptively: "community-center-mural.jpg", "school-mural.jpg", etc.

/portfolio/canvas/
  - Add your canvas artwork photos here

/portfolio/restorations/
  - Add before/after restoration photos here

/about/
  - profile.jpg - Your photo for the About section


AFTER ADDING IMAGES:
1. Update the portfolio items in: components/Portfolio.tsx
2. The site will auto-refresh to show changes


IMAGE TIPS:
- Use .jpg for photos, .png for logos with transparency
- Recommended portfolio image size: 800x800 pixels (square works best)
- Hero image: 1920x1080 or larger
- Logo: 200-400px wide
